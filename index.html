The Dark Knight

Batman is the superhero protector of Gotham City, a tortured, brooding vigilante dressed as a bat who fights against evil and strikes fear into the hearts of criminals everywhere.

Real Name

Bruce Wayne

Base

Gotham City

Abilities

Genius-level intellect, Master detective, Peak human physical condition, Martial arts master

Orphaned as a child when his parents were murdered before his eyes, Bruce Wayne traveled the world training himself to intellectual and physical perfection and learning a variety of crime-fighting skills.

BATMOBILES & VEHICLES

The Dark Knight's impressive collection of custom vehicles

The Tumbler

Heavily armored tactical assault vehicle capable of jumps without needing a ramp. Features include stealth mode, missile launchers and dual front machine guns.

Batpod

High-powered motorcycle with mounted cannons, deployed as an escape vehicle from the damaged Tumbler with ability to perform extreme maneuvers.

The Bat

Advanced aerial vehicle with VTOL capabilities, armed with machine guns and missiles, capable of precision maneuvering between city buildings.

ARSENAL & GADGETS

Cutting-edge technology designed by Wayne Enterprises

Batsuit

Kevlar bi-weave with reinforced joints. Lightweight, flexible armor plating.

Batarangs

Sharpened, metal, bat-shaped throwing weapons. Various specialized types available.

Detective Vision

Advanced HUD system that highlights enemies, weapons, and structural weaknesses.

Grapnel Gun

Fires a strong, retractable line used for scaling buildings quickly and efficiently.
